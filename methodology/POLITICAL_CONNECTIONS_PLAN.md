# Ontario Budget Beneficiaries: Political Connections Investigation Plan

## Objective
Map the individuals behind the companies receiving the largest Ontario government contracts and identify documented connections to political parties — particularly the governing Ontario PC Party and the Ford government.

## Research Methodology
For each category, investigate:
1. **Key individuals** — CEOs, founders, board members, owners
2. **Political donations** — via Elections Ontario records
3. **Registered lobbyists** — via Ontario Lobbyist Registry
4. **Revolving door** — former political staffers now working for/lobbying for these companies
5. **Personal connections** — fundraiser attendance, social ties, family connections
6. **Investigative reporting** — Globe and Mail, Toronto Star, The Narwhal, The Trillium, TVO, CBC investigations

## Research Categories (8 parallel investigations)

## Important Caveats
- Political donations are legal and public in Ontario
- Lobbying is legal and registered
- Having a lobbyist does not mean impropriety
- Connections ≠ corruption — the report will document facts, not make accusations
- Where information is not available, that will be stated clearly
