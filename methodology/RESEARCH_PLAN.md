# Ontario Provincial Budget 2025-26: Detailed Spending Research Plan

## Objective
Identify WHO specifically gets paid from Ontario's $232.5B budget — named companies, organizations, and individuals — with contract values and sources.

## Budget Overview
- **Total Revenue:** $219.9B
- **Total Spending:** $232.5B
- **Deficit:** $14.6B
- **Infrastructure (this year):** $35B
- **Infrastructure (10-year plan):** $223.1B

## Known Transparency Gaps
- Ontario does NOT publish a consolidated vendor-by-payment-amount list
- Hospital-level operating funding allocations are not public (per FAO)
- Procurement data is scattered across IO, Metrolinx, Supply Ontario, and ministry portals
- Consulting contract totals are not aggregated like federal data
- The Public Accounts Detailed Schedules of Payments (PDF) is the closest to a full list
