# 2. THE SKILLS DEVELOPMENT FUND SCANDAL

The $2.5B Skills Development Fund is the clearest documented case of the Ford government directing public money toward political allies.

## What the Auditor General Found

- Minister David Piccini's office overrode bureaucratic rankings, approving **$742M (56% of total)** to applicants ranked medium, low, or poor
- **64 low/medium-ranked projects** that hired lobbyists received **$126M**
- The fund was **"not fair, transparent or accountable"**
- **80% of for-profit SDF money** ($180M of $224M) under Piccini went to PC donor-led companies
- 35 for-profit recipients made their **first-ever PC donation after the SDF was created**

Sources: [CBC](https://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/toronto/ontario-auditor-general-skills-training-funding-1.7648124), [The Trillium](https://www.thetrillium.ca/news/the-trillium-investigations/labour-minister-has-given-pc-donors-companies-80-of-for-profit-businesses-skills-development-funds-11746974)

# 3. THE GREENBELT SCANDAL & HIGHWAY 413

## The Greenbelt Land Swap

Ford removed 7,400 acres from the Greenbelt. Five sites (owned by TACC/De Gasperis, Flato/Rehmatullah, and Fieldgate) made up **92% of all land removed**. The AG estimated the De Gasperis/Rice lands increased in value by nearly **$8 billion**.

|-----------|-------------|---------------|-------------------|----------|
| **De Gasperis family / TACC** | **$294,000+** to PCs | $50K (via Nashville Developments) | Multiple sites removed; $8B value increase | Peter Van Loan (former federal Conservative House Leader), Frank Klees (former PC MPP) |
| **Flato Developments (Shakir Rehmatullah)** | Attended stag-and-doe + Ford wedding | — | Land removed | — |
| **Mattamy Homes (Peter Gilgan)** | $61K to PCs | **$100K to Ontario Proud** | MZO benefits; $105M hospital donation | — |

Sources: [National Observer](https://www.nationalobserver.com/2021/04/03/investigations/developers-ties-ford-government-benefit-highway-413), [CBC](https://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/toronto/greenbelt-land-swap-wake-report-1.6960488), [The Narwhal](https://thenarwhal.ca/ontario-greenbelt-scandal/)

## Highway 413

Eight powerful developers own ~3,300 acres along the route. Combined donations: **~$753K to PCs + ~$60K to Ontario Proud**. Lobbyists include former Conservative politicians (Peter Van Loan, Frank Klees) and Ford insiders (Amir Remtulla — Rob Ford's former chief of staff).

Source: [National Observer](https://www.nationalobserver.com/2021/04/03/investigations/developers-ties-ford-government-benefit-highway-413)

# 4. LONG-TERM CARE

## The Harris-to-Ford Pipeline

The LTC-politics connection spans decades:

**1995-2002 (Harris government):**
- Eliminated minimum care standard (2.25 hrs/resident/day)
- Allocated majority of new beds to for-profit corporations

**2003-present (Harris post-premiership):**
- **Mike Harris** joined **Chartwell** as Board Chair in 2003, earning **~$3.5M** over 18 years, plus $7M in stock holdings
- Former Premier **Bill Davis** chaired Revera's predecessor

**2020 (COVID-19 — Ford government):**
All three major LTC chains hired Ford insiders as lobbyists:

| Lobbyist | Ford Connection | LTC Client | Registered |
|----------|----------------|------------|------------|
| **Melissa Lantsman** | 2018 campaign spokesperson, war room director, PC VP | **Extendicare** | Apr 2020 |
| **Leslie Noble** | Harris campaign manager (1995, 1999); donated $11,434 to PCs | **Chartwell + OLTCA** | Mar 2020 |
| **Lauren McDonald** | Ford's Director of Marketing | **Revera** | May 2020 |
| **Michael Wilson** | Chief of Staff to Attorney General (Ford gov't) | **Revera** | May 2020 |
| **Amir Remtulla** | Rob Ford's Chief of Staff | **Revera** | 2019-2020 |
| **Andrew Brander** | Worked for Minister Rod Phillips; $4,303 to PCs | **OLTCA** | During pandemic |

Sources: [Ontario NDP](https://www.ontariondp.ca/news/pandemic-began-all-three-big-ltc-corporations-have-hired-ford-insiders-lobby-government), [HuffPost](https://www.huffpost.com/archive/ca/entry/ontario-nursing-home-lobbyists-pc-party-donations_ca_5fc53f95c5b63d1b770e8a4d)

**Result:** Ford passed **Bill 218** giving LTC operators retroactive legal immunity from COVID lawsuits — while for-profit homes had **96% more COVID cases and 78% more deaths** than non-profit homes (CMAJ peer-reviewed study). Then awarded 75%+ of new beds to the same for-profit chains.

Sources: [CMAJ](https://www.cmaj.ca/content/192/33/E946), [Ontario Health Coalition](https://www.ontariohealthcoalition.ca/index.php/release-ford-government-is-awarding-thousands-of-long-term-care-beds-to-for-profit-chains-with-ghastly-records-of-death-and-suffering-new-coalition-report/)

## Alstom — The Sole-Source Deal

**Alstom** is the only transit company with extensive multi-layered lobbying (3+ consultant lobbyists via Edelman, plus in-house) AND the only one that received a **sole-source contract** ($2.3B TTC trains). Ford and Transportation Minister Sarkaria personally pushed for the deal, citing "Buy Canadian" amid tariffs.

Source: [CBC](https://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/toronto/ttc-alstom-train-deal-1.7609756)

## Most International Transit Firms

Most international consortium members (Ferrovial, VINCI, Webuild, Strabag, Dragados, Ghella) showed **no Ontario lobbying registrations** — they won contracts through competitive IO/Metrolinx procurement. The Ontario Lobbyist Registry is less transparent/searchable than the federal registry.

# 11. SUMMARY: THE NETWORK MAP

## The Pattern

The research reveals a consistent, repeating cycle:

```
1. Ford insider leaves government
2. Launches lobbying firm
3. Signs up clients with business before the province
4. Clients donate to PC Party
5. Clients receive government contracts/grants/approvals
6. Cycle repeats
```

## Strongest Documented Connections (DIRECT)

| Connection | Evidence | $ Involved |
|-----------|----------|-----------|
| Ford insiders → SDF grants | 6 lobbying firms run by Ford insiders steered $100M+ to clients; AG found process "not fair" | $742M in overridden approvals |
| Ontario Shipyards ↔ Teneycke ↔ Ford | Ford's campaign manager lobbies for company; Ford personally advocates for it; company donates to PCs | $22M SDF + $215M program |
| Greenbelt developers ↔ PC donations ↔ land removals | $753K to PCs + $60K Ontario Proud; AG found $8B windfall; RCMP investigating | $8B+ in land value |
| LTC operators ↔ Ford insiders ↔ Bill 218 immunity | 5 Ford insiders lobbied for LTC chains during COVID; legal immunity passed while residents died at 78% higher rate in for-profit homes | $6B+ annual LTC funding |
| AtkinsRealis ↔ Todd Smith | Hired sitting PC Energy Minister as VP after he oversaw nuclear policy | $2.1B Pickering + $450M Darlington + $1.3B Bruce MCR |
| Metrolinx board ↔ Conservative fundraising | Board chair also chairs Conservative Fund; Harris chief of staff on board | $60.7B 10-year transit plan |
