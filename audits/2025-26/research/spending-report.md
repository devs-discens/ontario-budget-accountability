Major operators: **Stock Transportation, First Student, Sharp Bus Lines, Voyago**. $40M+ for driver retention.

Source: [School Bus Ontario](https://schoolbusontario.ca/student-transportation-funding/)

## Universities — Provincial Operating Grants: $3.7B (2023-24)

Ontario provides the **lowest per-student university funding in Canada**: $10,246/FTE vs. national average $16,756. Province announced **$6.4B additional** over 4 years (2025) plus $750M for STEM. U of T total budget is $3.52B but provincial grants are only ~20% (~$700M).

Source: [FAO Postsecondary Review](https://fao-on.org/en/report/estimates-2025-pse/)

## Colleges — Provincial Operating Grants: $1.48B (2023-24)

Also lowest in Canada: $10,910/FTE vs. $16,002 national average.

## OSAP: $1.36B in grants (2023-24), serving 494,000 students

**Major 2026 change:** Grant-to-loan ratio reversed — max 25% grants / 75% loans (was the inverse).

Source: [OSAP](https://www.ontario.ca/page/osap-ontario-student-assistance-program)

# 10. DEBT INTEREST — $16.2B

## Ontario's Debt Profile

- **Net debt (2024-25):** $427.1B → **$457.5B** (2025-26) → $511.4B (2027-28)
- **Interest cost:** $16.2B (2025-26), rising to $19.8B by 2029-30
- **Borrowing program (2025-26):** $42.5B
- Ontario = **74.7% of all Canadian provincial bond trading**

## Who Gets the $16.2B?

Bond dealers (underwriters):
- **BMO Capital Markets, CIBC World Markets, National Bank Financial, RBC Capital Markets, Scotia Capital, TD Securities** — the Big Six Canadian banks
- Plus international: **Goldman Sachs, JP Morgan, Barclays**
- In Jun 2025, Ontario **excluded American banks** from a US$2B issuance for the first time since 2011

Likely major bondholders receiving interest:
- **Canadian pension funds:** OTPP ($247.5B assets), HOOPP ($123B), OMERS ($128.6B), OPTrust, CPPIB
- **Insurance companies:** Manulife, Sun Life, Canada Life
- **Banks and asset managers:** via bond funds and proprietary holdings
- **Foreign investors:** record $10.8B purchased in Jan 2025 alone
- Ontario bonds = **12.1% of FTSE Universe Bond Index** (so every Canadian bond fund holds them)

**Ontario does not disclose specific bondholders.** This is standard for government bonds globally.
