## Key Findings

1. **The SDF program was quietly erased.** Ten contracts worth $134M in our dataset — several under investigation — are not mentioned in the 2026 Budget. The spending continues under "Demand-Driven Employment and Training Programs" without the SDF brand.

2. **Four major energy projects announced with zero cost estimates.** Wesleyville nuclear, Greenstone transmission, Barrie-Sudbury transmission, and Darlington underwater line. Combined jobs claims of 26,500. All will flow through crown agencies outside Public Accounts visibility.

3. **Building Ontario Fund is a new procurement channel.** The BOF co-invests with private partners, bypassing normal procurement processes. $516.6M in new investments announced in this budget alone. The fund's investment decisions are not subject to the same transparency as IO or Metrolinx procurements.

4. **Highway 413 and Bradford Bypass advancing without cost disclosure.** Both have awarded design/construction contracts. Both have full-page spreads with maps. Neither discloses a total project cost.

5. **Invest Ontario Fund is disbursing hundreds of millions** to individual companies ($90M to Lee Li Holdings alone). The fund's decision-making process and any lobbying involvement should be examined.

6. **12 new companies should be added to the tracked list** for cross-referencing against lobbyist registry, election donations, and political connections.
